Setup

If you land on this page, it means that you have a setup to do. Don't worry, it won't take long. Just read carefully and follow the steps:

1. Creating a profile repository:

To submit posts on GitLounge, you need to have a public repository named after your username. So, take your time and create your repository however you like it.
